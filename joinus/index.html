Infomics Lab is recruting

We are looking for postdocs and students excited about computational challenges in genomics, transcriptomics, single cell analysis, genome editing and network analysis.

The ideal candidate should have received (or expect to receive soon) a Ph.D. in Computer Science, Statistics, Genetics, Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, Mathematics, Physics, or related fields.

The lab can also host visiting Phd students interested in collaborative projects on shared research topics of the lab.

If you are interested, please send your CV and a cover letter describing your current and future research interests, and the contact of 2-3 references to our CEO prof.Rosalba Giugno:

rosalba.giugno@univr.it
