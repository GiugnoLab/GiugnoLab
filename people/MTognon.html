Manuel Tognon
PhD student

Manuel Tognon is a PhD student in Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science, University of Verona. He received is BA in Bioinformatics and MA in Medical Bioinformatics at the University of Verona. His research focus on the development of computational methods to model and predict the epigenetic mechanisms regulating the gene expression in human genome, modeling and discovery of regulatory DNA motifs, and new imaging genetics workflows to integrate genetic and imaging information. For his PhD, he is co-supervised by prof. Rosalba Giugno (University of Verona) and Dr. Luca Pinello (Harvard Medical School). In his free time, he enjoys visiting fine arts museums, spending time with his two cats and watching football (let's go Pats!!!).
