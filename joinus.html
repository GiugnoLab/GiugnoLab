Grant: "SErvizi di gestione ed accreditamento di LABoratori biomolecolari (SELAB)"

A public selection is announced for qualifications and interview, for the conferment of n. 1 (one) scholarship of the duration of 12 (twelve) months each and renewable for subsequent 6 (six) months, for a total of 18 (eighteen) months), for young graduates in the Computer Science discipline, to be started at a training course from title "Management and accreditation of biomolecular LABORATORS (SELAB)", with the aim of create a team of nine professionals (4 Biologists, 2 Veterinaries, 1 Bioinformatics, 1 Management Engineer; 1 Information Engineer), experts in the management and accreditation of Biomolecular laboratories. The training is aimed at placing in the context of the implementation and management of a multi-center research service, ie creating a spin-off to offer advice in the field of research in personalized medicine or in the search for sources of funding and, again, in support for accreditation procedures of biomedical laboratories. The locations for carrying out the activities foreseen by the project will be:
- Experimental Zooprophylactic Institute of Sicily "A.Mirri" (IZSSi), Via Gino Marinuzzi, 3 - 90129 Palermo and the other territorial offices of the Institute.
- Institute of Molecular Bioimaging and Physiology of the National Research Council (IBFM-CNR), Secondary Headquarters of Cefalu', Contrada Pietrapollastra - Pisciotto snc- 90015 Cefalu' (PA) and institutions affiliated for research and / or training activities with the IBFM-CNR.
